BandMate

Where Musicians meet

Open the app

BandMate is a social networking app designed to help musicians and music lovers make connections, friends, an most importantly, great music.

For creatives, By creatives

"In the old days, you had to hang out at music stores and hope to find the right people. Things have changed now"

Bill Jackson

How it works

Create a profile that defines you with a series of vivid descriptions
Use a variety of search options and filters to look for potential connections, partners, and friends. When you find someone you're interested in, like their profile.
Once the person who's profile you liked likes you back, get in touch through the in app chat and start discussing plans and interests.
Yes, it's really that simple! Utilizing similar technology to hookup apps like tinder, we've created the ultimate music networking app for anybody, from beginners to long time vets.
